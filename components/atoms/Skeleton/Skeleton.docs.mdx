## Accessibility

- Renders with `role="status"` and `aria-busy="true"` — announces "Loading" to screen readers when it appears
- Use `aria-label` to provide context-specific announcements (e.g. `aria-label="Loading user profile"`)
- The shimmer animation respects `prefers-reduced-motion` — motion is suppressed for users who prefer less animation
- No keyboard interaction — display-only component
